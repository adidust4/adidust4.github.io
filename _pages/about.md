I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the <a href='usc.edu'>University of Southern California</a>. I work with <a href="https://maja-mataric.web.app/">Prof. Maja Matarić</a> in the <a href='https://uscinteractionlab.web.app/'>Interaction Lab</a> and <a href="https://www.chla.org/profile/pat-levitt-phd">Prof. Pat Levitt</a> in the Children's Hospital of Los Angeles' Developmental Neuroscience <a href='https://www.chla.org/research/lab/levitt'>Levitt Lab</a>. My research focuses on the use of machine learning and statistics to understand child development, specifically the impacts of mother-child interaction on child development.

I recieved a BA in Computer Science, with minors in Statistics and Sociology, from <a href='macalester.edu'>Macalester College</a> in 2023, advised by <a href="https://www.abbymarsh.com/">Prof. Abby Marsh</a> and <a href="https://jpena831.github.io/">Prof. Joslenne Peña</a>. I also worked in robotics research at the Rochester Institute of Technology, studying the Deaf and hard-of-hearing commuunity's interaciton with robots.
