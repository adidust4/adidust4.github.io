I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. I work with Maja Matarić in the <a href='https://uscinteractionlab.web.app/'>Interaction Lab</a> and Pat Levitt in the Children's Hospital of Los Angeles' Developmental Neuroscience <a href='https://www.chla.org/research/lab/levitt'>Levitt Lab</a>. My research focuses on the use of machine learning and statistics to understand child development, specifically the impacts of mother-child interaction on child development.

I recieved a BA in Computer Science, with minors in Statistics and Sociology, from <a href='macalester.edu'>Macalester College</a> in 2023, advised by Abby Marsh and 
Joslenne Peña. I also worked in robotics research at the Rochester Institute of Technology, studying the Deaf and hard-of-hearing commuunity's interaciton with robots.
